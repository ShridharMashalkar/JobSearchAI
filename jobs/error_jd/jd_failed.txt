Organization

Consulate General of Israel, Bengaluru (MASHAV – Israel’s Agency for International Development Cooperation)

Job Title

Education Fellowship Program Manager – MASHAV Initiatives

Location

Bengaluru, India (Hybrid; occasional travel within India and potential short-term training in Israel)

Job Type

Fellowship / Contract (6–12 months)

Role Summary

The Consulate General of Israel in Bengaluru is seeking a passionate and organized Education Fellowship Program Manager to lead and coordinate MASHAV-supported education and capacity-building initiatives across South India.

This role focuses on program execution, stakeholder engagement, and educational impact, working closely with academic institutions, government bodies, and international experts to deliver high-quality training programs.

Key Responsibilities
Plan, coordinate, and execute education-focused fellowship and training programs
Liaise with universities, NGOs, and government agencies to implement initiatives
Manage end-to-end fellowship lifecycle (applications, selection, onboarding, completion)
Organize workshops, seminars, and capacity-building sessions
Facilitate communication between Israeli experts and Indian partner institutions
Monitor program progress and ensure smooth execution of activities
Prepare program reports, documentation, and communication materials
Support outreach efforts to expand participation in MASHAV initiatives
Required Skills
Strong organizational and project management skills
Excellent communication and stakeholder management abilities
Experience in education programs, fellowships, or training initiatives
Ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously
Proficiency in MS Office tools (Word, PowerPoint, Excel)
Strong written and spoken English
Preferred Qualifications
Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Education, Public Policy, International Relations, or related field
Experience working with NGOs, embassies, or international organizations
Interest in global development, education, and cross-cultural collaboration
Perks & Benefits
Work on international education and development initiatives
Exposure to Indo-Israel collaboration programs
Opportunity to engage with global educators and experts
Certificate of completion and professional recognition
Potential participation in short-term training programs in Israel
Application Process
Submit CV + Statement of Purpose
Shortlisting based on profile and motivation
Interview with program coordinators
Final selection
